Tale Tiles

TALE TILES is an interactive web-based educational game designed to help students and literature enthusiasts learn about literary terms, theories, authors, and famous works through engaging visual puzzles.

Features of TaleTiles

  • Interactive Puzzle Gameplay: Each level provides four image clues related to a literary term or figure.
  • Letter Bank System: Players must deduce the correct answer by placing letters in order.
  • Scoring Mechanic: Start with 100 points per level; lose 10 points each time you remove a letter to try again.
  • Leaderboard: Submit your final score after completing all levels and view the top 10 scores in real-time.
  • Audio Feedback: Enjoy typewriter and button click sounds for enhanced gameplay immersion.
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Goal of the Project

The goal of TaleTiles is to bridge the gap between learning and fun by make learning literature enjoyable and accessible through gamification — blending education with fun gameplay to improve retention and interest in literary concepts.
